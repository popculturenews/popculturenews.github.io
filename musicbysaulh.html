About Saul H.


Saul Hirshberg, an individual commonly referred to by the alias "Saul H.", is an electronic child artist, drummer, amateur computer scientist, unprofessional author, and poet. He is a terribly quintessential producer of what he would consider "IDM", an alternative to traditional "EDM" compositions. His previous album, "Hello Pruchacho" is admittedly rather quirky, yet remains to incorporate a plethora of unique components to each individual track. He believes that his work is tremendous, yet he is not one to be modest. He is currently working on a variety of songs, some which he believes are more iconic than others. Saul received a diagnosis of "Autism Spectrum Disorder" at the age of approximately 2, yet he claims that he refers to his diagnosis as "Aspergers' Disorder", rather than the traditional (as he receives very mild symptoms). Amidst his career, he also analyzes chart data, and incorporates this information into CrownNote and Pulse (two applications for music sorts). Saul's music is provided on the Spotify application, Apple Music, and what not! I suppose that I'm approaching the closing of this autobiography. - Saul Hirshberg
