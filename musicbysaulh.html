About Saul H.


Saul Hirshberg (a.k.a Saul H.) is an independent intelligent dance musician and drummer. Listen to his new "This Album Has Infinite Titles/The Saul H. Album" EP NOW! This EP consists of songs Come On DJ, Raise The Roof, Mic Drop, Crescendo 2, Crescendo 3, Motivation, Mysterious, Ahh, An Interesting Composition, fLUOXETINE 3756, iCrate1542DD, I Could Experiment Forever (And I Will), 5/29/22shortSongUnfinished, and the long awaited completely Intelligent Dance Music track, symphony1composition14bysaul. The evolution of Saul's singles within this album have traveled a long way. During his early years, Saul used very simple piano melodies and relatively complex drum beats. He now uses well-produced, noticeably complex piano and drum melodies with his fLUOXETINE 3756 - symphony1composition14bysaul tracks. Listen to the first track on Saul's "Advanced Compositions/Symphonies Compilation/Album" EP, "best song #1". As the title states, this is by far Saul's "best song" in the eyes of an Intelligent Dance Music enthusiast. Saul was an autistic, musically gifted child at a young age, and progressed to start taking drum lessons at "The Prindle School in Hadley, MA" by age 7.
