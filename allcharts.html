Key:


Pos. or Peak Pos.

means the postion or rank that the song recieved on its "peak". The "peak" means the consecutive time spent at a postition on the chart.


Rank@___

means the position that an individual single on another chart. The "rank" indicates the position of the song in the chart.


lw

is an acronym for "last week". It means what "rank" that individual single had the week before this current chart volume. If the lw section appers as _, it means that it was not on the chart last week.


Wks.

explains how many weeks an individual single has spent on the "Top 50" Chart.
