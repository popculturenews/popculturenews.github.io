What Is All Charts Included?

All Charts Included is an independent chart format that incorporates charts that measure mainstream data throughout airplay, sales, and streaming. The Mediabase chart is udes to measure Airplay. Though, the Hot 100 chart is used to measure U.S. Sales.
The National Spotify (Global Top 50) Chart is a significant part of the streaming data, while the Deezer and iTunes charts also serve as major sources.


Pos. or Peak Pos.

means the postion or rank that the song recieved on its "peak". The "peak" highest point of the song's career on the chart.


Rank@___

means the position that an individual single has on another chart. The "rank" indicates the position of the song in the chart.


lw

is an acronym for "last week". It simply means the "rank" that the song has recieved the voliume before the current volume.


Wks.

indicates how many weeks an individual single has spent on the Big Picture Chart.
